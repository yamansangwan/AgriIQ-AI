# AgriIQ AI

AgriIQ AI is an agricultural intelligence platform that acts like a digital agronomist. It uses multiple AI agents to analyze crop photos and generate custom farming strategies in seconds.

## The Problem
Farming is unpredictable. Farmers deal with sudden weather changes and fast-spreading crop diseases. Usually, by the time a farmer realizes their crop is sick or weather is threatening their yield, it's too late to fix it.

## Our Solution
We bring expert farming advice directly to a smartphone or laptop. A farmer simply uploads a photo of a plant. Our AI immediately figures out the plant's health, checks the local weather forecast, and gives the farmer a day-by-day action plan on what to do next.

## Key Features
- **Crop Vision:** Looks at a photo and identifies the plant, its growth stage, and any early signs of sickness.
- **Disease Protection:** Tells you exactly how to treat the plant (with organic or chemical options).
- **Weather Intelligence:** Checks local weather data to warn against upcoming droughts, floods, or high humidity.
- **Farm Planner:** Gives you a weekly schedule for watering, fertilizing, and harvesting based on all the data above.
- **Impact Simulator:** Lets you ask "What if?" questions (like "What if it rains tomorrow?") and instantly updates your plan.

## Tech Stack
We built this to be fast and easy to deploy:
- **Frontend:** Next.js (React) and Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Node.js and Express
- **AI Engine:** Google Gemini 2.0 Flash
- **Storage:** ImageKit (for handling image uploads)

## Use Cases
- **Saving Sick Crops:** A farmer spots a yellowing leaf, takes a picture, and finds out it's a simple nitrogen deficiency before it ruins the whole field.
- **Adapting to Weather:** A farm manager uses the tool to realize heavy rain is coming, so they skip watering for the day to save money and prevent root damage.
- **Going Green:** A traditional farmer uses the AI to find sustainable, organic alternatives to the harsh chemicals they usually use.
